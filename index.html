About Theos

Built for South African SMMEs, not template clients.

We're an AI-powered business agency based in Randfontein, working with small and growing businesses across South Africa. We handle the parts that are hard to do alone: a website that converts, a brand that looks the part, and automation that quietly runs in the background.

Past work includes financial reporting pipelines, WhatsApp-driven client funnels, and compliance support for CIPC registration and SEDA / SEDFA funding pathways — built on lean, low-cost tool stacks so the systems pay for themselves.
